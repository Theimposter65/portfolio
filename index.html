THE STORY

FORGED BY FIRE.

I am Mr Imposter — a B.Tech student in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Silicon University, Bhubaneswar, driven by an unyielding passion for technology and innovation.

At the intersection of hardware and software, I thrive — tinkering with circuits, writing code, and relentlessly optimizing both until they perform at their absolute best. For me, engineering is not just a discipline; it is a way of thinking.

My journey has not been without adversity. In 2016, I lost my father to cancer — a defining moment that reshaped my perspective on life. Since then, I have stood beside my mother, aware of the weight she carries, and committed to building a future that eases her burden. Every late night spent studying, every problem solved, every skill acquired — it is all in service of something greater than myself.

I do not just learn technology. I use it with purpose.
